Pasta is one of my favorite foods EVER!


  1. Tortellini

    • Stuffed pasta
    • Usually filled with cheese or meat

  2. Fettuccine

    • Flat thick noodles
    • Great with Alfredo sauce

  3. Bowtie

    • Fun shape
    • Good for pasta salad

How to make Pasta:

  • Bring water to a boil
  • Pour in pasta
  • Cook until soft
  • Drain with strainer
  • Add sauce and enjoy
